I am a research scientist at General AI Lab, Horizon Robotics. I got my Bachelor of Computer Science from Peking University in 2011, and my Ph.D. of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) from Purdue University in 2016. Previously, I worked at Baidu Research (2016-2018) and Facebook AI Research (2018-2019).

My earlier research topics include object segmentation [ACMMM2010], language grounding from video [ACL2013,AAAI2015,JAIR2015,IJCV2017], video captioning [CVPR2016], embodied AI [ICLR2018,CoRL2018,arXiv2019], and the lottery ticket hypothesis [NeurIPS2019,ICLR2020].

Since joining Horizon Robotics in 2019, I've been working on reinforcement learning (RL) problems, including hierarchical RL [ICLR2021,NeurIPS2021], safe/constrained RL, and exploration. I am also an active maintainer of the RL framework ALF.
