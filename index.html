I am a research scientist at General AI Lab, Horizon Robotics. Previously, I worked at Baidu Research and Facebook AI Research.

My early research focused on various topics such as object segmentation, language grounding in video, video captioning , embodied language understanding, and the lottery ticket hypothesis. Since joining Horizon Robotics in 2019, I've been working on learning-based robotics problems, for example, hierarchical RL, safe/constrained RL, and exploration.

I love writing code and building things. I am the co-author and an active maintainer of the RL framework ALF that supports most RL research at Horizon.

Research interests

My current passion is to combine all my past experience on language grounding, video understanding, and learning-based robotic control, to build an agile robot that takes natural language commands in indoor scenarios with strong generalization capabilities.
